Friend or Threat

Artificial Intelligence, unveiling a new horizon, where possibilities.
boundless, stepping onto the path of progress. If it arrives as a friend,.
life will become easier, providing new vigor in solving problems. However,
if it becomes a threat, it can take away jobs, creating discord and
adversity. Uncontrolled AI can bring disaster, casting a dark shadow over
the existence of humankind. Therefore, caution and proper use are
necessary, binding it with the ties of ethics, its reform must be done.
Friend or threat, the answer to this question, depends on our use of it.
If driven on the right path, AI will be a friend, otherwise, it can become
a great enemy.
